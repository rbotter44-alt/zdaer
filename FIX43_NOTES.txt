fix43_pwa_ad_protection

Applied the user's PWAAdProtection idea around the VidFast iframe:
- No sandbox.
- Click Shield: first tap is swallowed and shows a toast.
- Touch unlock: iframe receives touches for 7 seconds, then relocks.
- Restore Guard: on focus/visibility return, it restores layout/shield.
- If the iframe navigates immediately after unlock, it restores the original VidFast URL and relocks.

This does not remove ads inside VidFast. It only tries to reduce first-click ad redirects and restore the player.
